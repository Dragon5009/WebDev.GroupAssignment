Roller Prime

Roller Prime is one of the main attractions at the Magical Kingdom. It is a 1.8km long roller coaster with a maximum speed of 110km/h. It is one of the fastest roller coasters in the world and is a must for any thrill seeker. It is a ride that will leave you speechless!

Event Space

We have a specialist event space, hosting a wide variety of events. Ranging from live band performances. Such as the upcoming Clamshell Singers, to training days and group activities. If you have an event you think would be perfect for our space, please get in touch with us!

Splashing Water River

Ever wanted to ride a river and experience the gentle flow with just a dash of excitement? Now you can! Our Splashing Water River ride is perfect for the whole family. It is a meandering river that will take you around several particularly beautiful areas of the park. There are a few spots where the pace picks up, but overall its a relaxing ride in nature.

Petting Zoo

Free to access as part of your visit, you have the unique oportunity to meet some of our animals. From our cuddly pigmy goat Toby to our friendly corn snake Mazie, there is a whole range of creatures eager to meet you, our knowledgable staff are on-hand to help you learn and interact with our animals, in a safe and educational way. No matter what your age or experience level is.
